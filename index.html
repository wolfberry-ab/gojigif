Everything you need to capture a GIF

🖥️

Region or whole screen

Draw a selection or grab the entire display. Resize with corner handles before you record.

👀

Live preview

A native-resolution preview that updates exactly when the screen changes — event-driven, never on a timer.

✋

Move while recording

Drag the capture region around mid-recording (size locked) to follow the action.

⏸

Pause & resume

Pause to skip the boring parts — paused time is excluded from the GIF's timing.

⏱

Timestamp & title card

Optionally burn the elapsed time into each frame, or open with a short title card.

▶

Adjustable FPS & playback

5–60 fps with real-timestamp timing. Play the last recording back in the app and reveal it in Finder.

🔒

🛡️

Signed & notarized

macOS builds are signed with our Apple Developer ID and notarized by Apple; Windows builds are signed too — both launch without scary warnings.

How it works

From screen to shareable GIF in three steps.

Pick what to record

Drag a region on the live preview, or switch on whole-screen capture.

Record

Hit Record. Move the region, pause/resume, and adjust FPS as you go.

Get your GIF

Stop, and GojiGif writes an animated GIF straight to your Desktop.
