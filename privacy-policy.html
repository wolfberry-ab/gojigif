Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

The short version: GojiGif does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. It has no telemetry, no analytics, and no account system, and it makes zero network calls. Your recordings and the GIFs you create never leave your device.

Who we are

GojiGif is a desktop application developed by Wolfberry AB, a software company based in Sweden. You can reach us at info@wolfberry.se.

What data we collect

None. GojiGif runs entirely on your computer. We do not collect, receive, or have access to any information about you, your device, or how you use the app.

Network connections

GojiGif makes no network requests in normal use. There is no update check, no license activation, no usage reporting, and no cloud component. The app works fully offline.

Your recordings and GIFs

When you record, frames are held in memory only for as long as needed to build the GIF, and the resulting GIF is written to a location you control (your Desktop by default). These files stay on your machine. We never see them, and the app never uploads them anywhere.

Permissions

On macOS, GojiGif requests the system Screen Recording permission, and only when you start capturing — it is required by the operating system to record the screen. The app does not request any permission it does not need.

Analytics, tracking & advertising

GojiGif contains no analytics SDKs, no trackers, no advertising, and no third-party code that phones home.

Children's privacy

GojiGif does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Downloads & this website

This website (gojigif.wolfberry.se) is hosted on GitHub Pages and the downloads are hosted on GitHub. When you visit this site or download the app, GitHub may process your IP address and request metadata as part of serving the content, under GitHub's privacy statement. This is outside our control and applies to any GitHub-hosted page. The app itself contacts no servers.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and publish the new version on this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@wolfberry.se. For security reports, see our security policy or email security@wolfberry.se.
